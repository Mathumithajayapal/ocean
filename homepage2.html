Innovative Solutions

Our core pillars for a healthier ocean – from cleanup to education.

Plastic Recovery

Advanced collection systems remove microplastics and debris from surface waters and coastlines.

Coral Restoration

Active reef rehabilitation using resilient coral nurseries and AI‑powered monitoring.

Ocean Intelligence

Satellite tracking & data analytics to map pollution hotspots and protect marine life.

Success Stories

Real impact from our projects around the world.

Restoration

Reviving the Great Barrier Reef

Using heat‑resistant coral strains, we restored over 12 hectares of degraded reef in just 18 months.

Turning the Tide on Plastic

A community‑led initiative in Indonesia removed 280 tonnes of ocean plastic and created 50 local jobs.
