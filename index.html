    🌊 Our Mission

    We combine cutting-edge technology, community action, and scientific research to reverse ocean damage and build a sustainable blue economy.

    Zero Waste

    Eliminate plastic leakage into marine ecosystems by 2030 through circular design.

    Restore Reefs

    Restore 1 million square meters of coral reef using resilient nursery techniques.

    AI Monitoring

    Real-time satellite & drone tracking to detect pollution and protect wildlife.
