    our story

    Born on the Open Water

    Oceanix was born on a small research vessel off the coast of Bali in 2020. What started as a 10‑day microplastic survey evolved into a global movement when we realized the scale of the crisis — and the power of community action.

    Today, we combine field science with open‑source technology to map ocean pollution in real time. Our crew includes marine biologists, data engineers, and former fishermen who know the sea better than anyone.

    We believe the ocean can heal if we give it a chance.

    Our Journey

    From a single boat to a global movement — every milestone brings us closer to a thriving ocean.

    2020

    First Voyage

    A small team of 6 sets sail from Bali to map microplastic pollution — the spark that ignited Oceanix.

    2021

    Global Network

    Partnerships with 12 research institutes across 5 countries launch our first coordinated cleanup campaigns.

    2022

    Tech Hub

    Our open‑source AI debris‑mapping platform goes live, empowering communities worldwide to track ocean pollution.

    2023

    Reef Revival

    We restore our first 3 coral reefs using innovative bio‑rock technology and community‑led planting.

    2025

    Million‑Mangrove Pledge

    We hit 12,000 mangroves planted and launch our pledge to restore 1 million by 2030 — with your help.

    Our Mission & Vision

    Mission

    To deploy science, technology, and community action to eliminate ocean plastic, restore marine habitats, and inspire a global movement for ocean stewardship.

    Vision

    A thriving, plastic‑free ocean where marine biodiversity flourishes and coastal communities live in harmony with the sea — for generations to come.
